About Us

Founded in 2019, Maharashtra Heat Treat is a trusted heat treatment service provider located in Pimpri-Chinchwad, Pune. We specialize in precision, quality, and reliability for a wide range of industrial applications. Our dedicated team and state-of-the-art machinery deliver tailor-made and timely solutions to meet diverse client needs.

Our Services

  • Carburising Hardening and Tempering: Multi-step process that achieves superior case depth and surface hardness, followed by tempering for enhanced toughness.
  • Only Carburising: Surface hardening by carbon enrichment to increase wear resistance.
  • Stress Relieving Annealing (SR): Removes internal stresses to improve stability and ductility.
  • Hardening and Tempering: Improves strength and reduces brittleness for industrial components.
  • Toughening: Special treatment to balance strength and toughness effectively.
  • All Types of Material: We handle gears, wheels, pinions, shafts, and many other components.
  • Tool Steel Treatment: Maximizing lifespan and wear resistance of tool steels.
